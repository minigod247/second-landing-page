About

disko is an emerging Afro-Soul artist focused on emotion, depth, and atmosphere. His music blends soulful melodies with darker tones, creating sound that reflects personal experiences, quiet struggles, and moments of reflection. Rather than chasing noise, the goal is simple: to create music that feels honest, timeless, and deeply human.

Every song is an attempt to translate feeling into sound: the things that are sometimes difficult to say out loud.
